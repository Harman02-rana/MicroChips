About MicroChip Cart
MicroChip Cart

A B2B and B2C electronic components marketplace for microchips, modules, sensors, and embedded hardware.

  • Browse products by category, brand, model, SKU, and product details.
  • Use search to find parts by name, module, SKU, brand, or category.
  • Add items to cart, save products to wishlist, and checkout with order details.
  • User accounts support orders, wishlist, profile settings, security, and theme preferences.
  • Distributor accounts support business buying, GST details, invoices, recommendations, and availability updates.
  • Returns, order history, community signals, and help tools are available from the store experience.
